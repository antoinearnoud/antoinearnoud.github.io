Biography

I am a PhD student in Economics at Yale University. My research focuses on how the interaction between technologies and public policies affect income and wealth inequality. Specifically, I study the impact of automation possibilities on wage bargaining. My research is at the intersection of public finance, labor economics and macroeconomics. I also develop computational tools that are useful in my work.
